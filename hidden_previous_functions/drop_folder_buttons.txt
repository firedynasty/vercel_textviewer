HIDDEN FUNCTIONALITY: Drop Folder Buttons
==========================================

Two buttons were hidden in src/components/ControlBar.js using style={{ display: 'none' }}.
The underlying logic and event handlers remain intact — they are only visually hidden.

--- Button 1: "Drop Folder Here" ---
- className: drop-folder-btn
- Triggers: folderInputRef (hidden <input type="file" webkitdirectory multiple>)
- Handler: handleFolderSelect
- Also supports drag-and-drop via: handleDragOver, handleDragLeave, handleDrop
- Behavior: Loads a folder recursively (all subfolders included)

--- Button 2: "Folder Flat" ---
- className: drop-folder-btn
- title: "Load folder without subfolders (depth 1)"
- Triggers: shallowFolderInputRef (hidden <input type="file" webkitdirectory multiple>)
- Also supports drag-and-drop via: handleDragOver, handleDragLeave, handleDropShallow
- Behavior: Loads only the top-level folder contents, no subfolders (depth 1)

--- How to restore ---
Remove style={{ display: 'none' }} from each button in ControlBar.js (~line 336 and ~line 396).
