# Introduction

Welcome to the **Static Content Viewer**!

This viewer supports:

- **Markdown files** (`.md`) - rendered with full formatting
- **Text files** (`.txt`) - displayed as plain text
- **Audio files** (`.mp3`, `.m4a`, `.wav`) - playable from the sidebar

## Features

1. Click on text/markdown files to view their content
2. Click on audio files to play them
3. Audio plays independently - you can view other content while listening
4. Keyboard navigation with arrow keys

    print("Hello, World!")
